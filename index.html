What I do üí™

  • I am detecting new customers entering my area, and I know where their hands are.
  • I accept orders for sweets and I am delivering them to you
  • I will ask you for money (not real one; in my shop we have only Baxter Banknotes)
  • I will accept your payment by recognising the money notes you gave me.

Money Recognition

I need to recognise banknotes, and my programmer tried few ideas (three in specific!) and he decided that AR Mark recognition is the best possible way to achieve a satisfactory result

Once I receive the customer's banknotes, I use my forehead camera to do the recognition as you can see from the pictures below.

Don't try to trick me in any way, I am well programmed to understand your tricks üòá

Lets see the world through my eyes üëÄ

I don't have eyes üòû but I do have cameras, I have three in total so I have more "eyes" than you do üòù

I use a depth sensor (Kinect-like sensor) to understand the positioning and the hands of the customers. These sensor makes possible to interact with you and being able to "see" exactly where you hand is in 3D space so I can get or give money.
